About

Faded Cities is a platform which showcases the architecture of cities isolated socially, politically or geographically in the 21st century. This was not always the case. These cities were often the site of architectural projects and visions when the outlook of the city was very different. We explore what these architects, designers and urbanists saw in these cities and the lasting legacy these projects have had on the city’s urban environment and its people. By studying and reflecting on each city’s architectural heritage, we hope to provide a framework for a sustainable model of urban development that takes into account every city’s unique social, historical and geographic context.
